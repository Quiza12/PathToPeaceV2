My Approach


I provide support in a 3-pronged approach which addresses all areas of your experience as a human being. If it helps you to understand, you can think of yourself as a car!

Psychological System

The understanding of the simplicity of our human experience, and the helpful mindset tweaks which can assist us in living purposeful lives.

Or, why we drive the car.

Biological System

The variety of factors that directly support your health, wellbeing, and overall function. For instance, exercise, nutrition, sleeping, breathing and other similar factors.

Or, how we fuel the car.

Systems and Skillsets

Systems are the habits we develop, while skillsets are the qualities we develop.

Or, how we drive the car.
